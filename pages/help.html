Help

Go to the index to see all your golinks.

The index allows you to list, create, edit, and delete your golinks.

There are two easy ways to quickly access the index:

  1. Type go/ in your browser's address bar and press Enter
  2. Pin the extension in your browser's toolbar, then click it's icon and select Options

Tips

The name of a golink will always be lowercase.

If you attempt accessing a golink that doesn't yet exist you'll be forwarded to create a new golink, go/example.

You can namespace golinks with slashes (go/gh/repos).
